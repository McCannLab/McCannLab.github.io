Monitoring Biodiversity and Biostructure

Kevin McCann  

June 12, 2016

  biodiversity biostructure biomonitoring

Tweet

A recent article by Dr. Bailey McMeans and others argues that there is a consistent response of biodiversity to human impacts, from individual level attributes to whole ecosystems. This integration across scales, seldom considered in a discipline often separated by scales suggests that there exists an overarching framework for biomonitoring ecosystems under human impact. The paper is is published in Ecological Monographs.
